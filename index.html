The fates are sealed

Forty-eight teams. Eight portfolios. May the best ball-watcher win.

The method

Four FIFA-seeded pots of twelve. Snake draft across six rounds — order randomised once, then 1→8, 8→1, 1→8 and so on. Everyone leaves with a balanced spread of giants, scrappers, and dark horses.

Why it's fair

No-one ends up with four no-hopers. No-one hoards the favourites. The snake means going first isn't a permanent advantage — pick last in Pot 1, pick first in Pot 2.

The reveal

Each pick spins through the remaining pot and locks on one. The result is deterministic — anyone watching this page sees exactly the same draw.
