    ★ Method ★

    Six Pots

    Forty-eight teams seeded into six pots of eight, ranked top to bottom. Pot 1 is your Frances and Brazils; Pot 6 is your Curaçaos and your Haitis.

    Snake Draft

    Order randomised once, then alternates direction each round. 1 → 8, 8 → 1, 1 → 8. Going first isn't a permanent advantage.

    Six Rounds

    One pot per round, drawn in order. Every player walks away with exactly one team from every pot. Perfectly balanced — no empires, no graveyards.

    Seeded Random

    The result is locked from the draw timestamp. Anyone watching this URL sees identical picks. The drama is in the reveal, not the maths.
